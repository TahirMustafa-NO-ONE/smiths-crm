Create admin pages for the following Smith & Co. CRM models to manage all business operations:

### Core Pages to Create (Priority Order):

1. **Client Pages** (CRUD Operations)
   - List all clients with filtering and pagination
   - View client details with associated contacts, projects, and interactions
   - Create new client with team member assignment
   - Edit/Update client information
   - Include team member field to assign responsible agent

2. **Team Member Pages** (CRUD Operations)
   - Manage team members and their roles
   - Assign team members to clients, campaigns, and projects
   - View team member workload and assigned items
   - Create, edit, and manage team member profiles
   - Include role/permission management

3. **Contact Pages** (CRUD Operations)
   - Manage client contacts
   - Link contacts to clients
   - Store contact information, communication history
   - Assign team members for contact management

4. **Lead Pages** (CRUD Operations)
   - Track and manage leads through the sales funnel
   - Assign leads to team members
   - Convert leads to clients/customers
   - Track lead source and status

5. **Campaign Pages** (CRUD Operations)
   - Create and manage marketing/sales campaigns
   - Assign team members to campaigns
   - Track campaign performance metrics
   - Link campaigns to clients and leads

6. **Project Pages** (CRUD Operations)
   - Manage client projects
   - Assign team members to projects
   - Track project progress and milestones
   - Link projects to clients

7. **Task Pages** (CRUD Operations)
   - Create and manage tasks
   - Assign tasks to team members
   - Track task status and deadlines
   - Link tasks to projects, campaigns, and contacts

8. **Customer Pages** (CRUD Operations)
   - Manage customer information
   - Track customer lifecycle
   - Assign customer service team members
   - Store customer communication history

### Universal Features for ALL Pages:
- ✅ Team member assignment field (dropdown/select)
- ✅ Search and filter functionality
- ✅ Pagination for large datasets
- ✅ Date filters (created_at, updated_at)
- ✅ Status filters and indicators
- ✅ Action buttons (View, Edit, Delete, Archive)
- ✅ Breadcrumb navigation
- ✅ Responsive design (mobile-friendly)
- ✅ Loading states and error handling
- ✅ Success/error notification toasts

### Dashboard Page:
- Overview of all key metrics
- Quick stats for clients, leads, projects, tasks
- Team member workload summary
- Recent activities and upcoming tasks
- Assigned items per team member
